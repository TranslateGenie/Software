Pricing

Each pack includes requests and character capacity. Access ends when either limit is reached. Applicable taxes not included in price.

  • ($10) One Wish: 10 requests or 200,000 characters
  • ($99) Two Wishes: 100 requests or 2,000,000 characters
  • ($350) Three Wishes: 500 requests or 10,000,000 characters
  • ($1250) Genie Sidekick: 2,000 requests or 40,000,000 characters
  • ($5000) Genie Companion: 10,000 requests or 200,000,000 characters

To get started, download the installer and activate your license key after purchase.
