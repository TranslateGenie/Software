Pricing

Each pack includes requests and character capacity. Access ends when either limit is reached. Applicable taxes not included in price.

  • ($99) Starter Pack: 100 requests or 2,000,000 characters
  • ($350) Small Org Pack: 500 requests or 10,000,000 characters
  • ($1250) Medium Org Pack: 2,000 requests or 40,000,000 characters
  • ($5000) Enterprise Pack: 10,000 requests or 200,000,000 characters
